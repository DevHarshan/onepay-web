Onepay was an idea put forth as a solution for a current social issue of having to go to a bank to make the payments manually. With the global pandemic, it was vital to maintain social distance. However, it has been quite a challenge in Sri Lanka to follow the protocol due to a lot of difficulties and issues in the current social system. Hence, the introduction of Onepay to help you with contactless payment making lives easier today and tomorrow.
